History

Here, I'm going to tell you about each RAC game. Starting from first one to the latest game released. I'm going to say the name of each game and what happened in each game as well.

Ratchet and Clank :

RAC 2002 (PS2 Original, PS3, PS Vita)

Ratchet and Clank is the beginning of the series. Clank land on Ratchet's home planet Veldin. Ratchet finds Clank. Clank then tells Ratchet about Chairman Drek (The main antagonist of the game) plan. Ratchet and Clank then travel around the galaxy to find Captain Qwark. Captain Qwark was working Drek the whole time and tried to kill Ratchet and Clank. Once Ratchet and Clank defeated Qwark, they went after Drek themselves. Using all the weapons and gadgets they collected through their travels, they managed to defeat Drek and put an end to his plan which was to destroy other planets and take the most famous places from those destroyed planets and fuse them into one epic planet to create his species a home.

Ratchet and Clank Going Commando :

RAC Going Commando 2003 (PS2, PS3, PS Vita)

The second installment to the RAC series, Ratchet is hired by a corporation name MegaCorp to get back a stolen experiment called the Protopet from a masked thief. Ratchet and Clank defeats the masked thief and get back the experiment. The two heroes were betrayed by Mr. Fizzwidget by destroying their ship and leaving them to die in a desert. While they were about to escape the desert, they were confronted by the masked thief, but this time the masked thief revealed herself. The thief is Angela Cross and she is a lombax like Ratchet. The Protopet is a dangerous experiment that kills people. Mr. Fizzwidget duplicated it and started selling the product. Ratchet and Clank and Angela went on to face Mr. Fizzwidget, but they were confronted by the Thug 4 Less group and they kidnapped Angela. After defeating the thugs and saving Angela, they went to the MegaCorp base to stop Fizzwidget once and for all. Little do our heroes know that he wasn't really Mr. Fizzwidget. Captain Qwark disguised as Fizzwidget and put the galaxy in danger and put the blame on Ratchet and Clank because they defeated him and he lost his hero rank. Qwark thought that if he put the galaxy in danger and save it, he could be a hero again. Qwark was then defeated by the Protopet. The Protopet became bigger than its original size because it was being tortured. Ratchet and Clank then defeated the Protopet and found the real Mr. Fizzwidget and found a device to fix all the Protopets that were released into the galaxy.
